💡 What is this?

Following our brilliant games night (congrats Team Middleton! 🏆), Uncle B has created something special for the kids.

CODE QUEST is a fun, game-like learning platform that teaches kids to build apps using AI. Think of it like Duolingo, but for coding with Claude AI.

The end goal? On Hackathon Day (date TBD), we'll have a family hackathon where each child (or team) builds their own quiz game and demos it to everyone. Then we all vote on our favourites!

"Vibe coding" is the concept - kids describe what they want to build in plain English, and AI helps them create it. No complex coding required!

❓ Quick FAQs

How long does the prep take?
About 30 mins total across all levels. They can do 5-10 mins at a time.
What age is this for?
8+ ideally, but younger kids can do it with a parent helping.
Is it safe?
Yes! They're just talking to Claude AI and building simple web pages. No downloads, no personal data collected beyond their chosen display name.
What if they get stuck?
Each level has copy-paste prompts they can use. And they can always ask Uncle B for help!
Do I need to know coding?
Nope! That's the beauty of "vibe coding" - the AI does the technical stuff.
