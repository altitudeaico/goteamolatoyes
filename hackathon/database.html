First — what's a database?

A database is like a super organised notebook that lives on the internet. Your quiz uses it to remember things — like all the questions, and who got the highest score.

Without a database, every time someone plays your quiz, the scores disappear. With a database, every score is saved forever so the whole family can compete! 🏆

🤔 Think of it like this...

📝
Without a database Like writing quiz scores on a piece of paper — it disappears when the page closes
💾
With a database Like writing scores in a permanent book online — saved forever, visible to everyone
❓
Quiz questions Stored in the database so the quiz can load them any time — even on different devices
🏆
Leaderboard All scores saved so the whole family can see who's winning — even days later!

🤝 Step 1 — Agree on your topic as a team

Before you talk to Claude, your whole team needs to agree on one topic for your quiz. It can be anything the whole family would enjoy playing!

Some ideas:

⚽ Football 🐾 Animals 🎬 Movies & TV 🎵 Music 🌍 Geography 🍕 Food 🦸 Superheroes 🎮 Video Games
💡 Team tip: Pick something the whole family can play — not just your team. The judges (your parents and relatives) need to be able to play it too on hackathon day!

🤖 Step 2 — Ask Claude to do everything

Go to claude.ai, start a new chat, and send this message. Fill in YOUR details:

I want to build a quiz game for my team. Please read these instructions carefully and do everything for me. Here are your instructions: [PASTE THE CONTENTS OF QUIZ_DB.md HERE] Here are my details: - My team is: [GIRLS or BOYS] - My quiz topic is: [YOUR TOPIC] - My Supabase service key is: [YOUR SERVICE KEY] - My GitHub token is: [YOUR GITHUB TOKEN] Please: 1. Create the quiz questions in the database (you can come up with 10 fun questions about my topic — show them to me first so I can approve them) 2. Build a fun colourful quiz game that loads the questions and saves scores 3. Put it online for me
✅ What Claude will do: Show you the questions first for approval, then save them to the database, build the quiz page, and deploy it — all automatically!
⚠️ Keep your keys secret! Don't share them with anyone or post them anywhere. Only paste them into Claude.

✅ Step 3 — Approve your questions

Claude will show you the 10 questions before saving them. This is your chance to:

  • 👍
    Say yes to the ones you like If the questions look good, just say "Those are great, go ahead!"
  • ✏️
    Ask Claude to change any you don't like Just say something like "Can you change question 3 to be about [something else]?" and Claude will swap it out.
  • ➕
    Add your own questions Say "Can you add a question about [something specific]?" and Claude will include it.

🚀 Step 4 — Your quiz goes live!

Once you approve the questions, Claude will:

  • 1
    Save all questions to the database So the quiz can load them on any device, any time.
  • 2
    Build your quiz game A colourful, fun page with your questions, answer buttons, instant feedback, and a score at the end.
  • 3
    Put it online automatically Your quiz will be live at a link like:
    altitudeaico.github.io/goteamolatoyes/hackathon/girls-quiz.html
  • 4
    Share it with the family! Send the link in the WhatsApp group so everyone can practise before hackathon day 🎮
