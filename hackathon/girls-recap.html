What happened

Three Sessions. One Live Quiz. 🛠️

The Girl Power team had THREE sessions across two days — and by the end they had a real multiplayer quiz that anyone in the world can play. Let's go! 🎉

☀️

Session 1 — Elsie & Uncle B ☀️

Thursday 2nd April · 9:17am · Morning build session

Set everything up from scratch! GitHub is where the code lives online. Supabase is where all the quiz answers and scores get saved. Both up and running! ✅
Team name sorted right away: Girl Power. 💅 The brand was decided before a single line of code was written!
Used Claude to design exactly how the quiz should look and feel — pastel colours, glitter, sparkles, butterflies, and confetti every time you get an answer right! 🦋✨
Claude's first design came back too dark. Elsie said "nope!" and told it exactly what to do differently — brighter colours, fashion pictures, and more sparkles. She was the boss! 👑
Made sure the quiz questions were about things Elsie actually knows and loves — fashion, art, makeup, and cheerleading. No boring questions here! 💃
Next steps: share the login details with Hannah, double-check Hannah's email address, and update the Claude project name. 📋
🌙

Session 2 — All Three Girls Together! 🌙

Thursday 2nd April · 5:55pm · Evening team session

The microphones were playing up at the start and nobody could hear each other properly! But they sorted it out and kept going. That's teamwork! 🎤
Played through the quiz together — and it was actually working! Scores were being saved. Multiple people could play at the same time. It was REAL! 🎮
The timer was too fast — everyone kept running out of time! So they made a big decision: get rid of the timer completely. Now the quiz just waits for everyone to finish. Much better! ⏱️
Elsie's job after the meeting: make the final tweaks to her quiz AND the team version for Sophia and Hannah too. She had homework! 📝
🏁

Session 3 — Hannah, Sophia & Uncle B 🏁

Saturday 4th April · Hackathon Day · Final catch-up!

Hannah and Sophia joined on the morning of the hackathon itself! Talk about dedication — still working on it on the big day! 💪
Hannah had already built her own quiz using Claude — it was really good! Aunty Lina helped download it and email the file across so it could go online. 📧
A bug was found where the "I'm hosting" and "I'm playing" buttons weren't working. Uncle B fixed it — and fixed 5 more bugs on top of that! 🐛→✅
The multiplayer quiz was tested live — one screen as host, another as player. It worked! Everyone could see the same questions at the same time. 🎮
The finished quiz went live at a real website address that anyone can visit. The Girl Power quiz is officially on the internet! 🌐✨
Uncle B was so impressed he suggested doing a whole Code Quest 2 next time — this is just the beginning! 🚀
