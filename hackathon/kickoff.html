The takeaways

What We Learned 🧠

1

AI is your superpower

You don't need to know how to code. You just need imagination and clear words. If you can describe it, Claude can build it.

2

A prompt is just talking to the AI

The words you give to Claude are your prompt. The clearer and more detailed your words, the better Claude's output will be.

3

You're building two things

By hackathon day, each team will have built a personal webpage AND a quiz game that the whole family can play. Both made entirely with AI.

4

Heroes are stronger together

The Avengers. The Justice League. Team Olatoye. When you combine your ideas and creativity, you build something none of you could do alone.

5

You only need imagination and words

Not a computer science degree. Not years of coding experience. Just the ability to imagine something and describe it clearly. You all have that already.
