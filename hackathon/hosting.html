What does "putting it online" mean?

Right now your webpage only exists on your screen — nobody else can see it. Putting it online gives it a real link on the internet, like a proper website, so the whole family can visit it from anywhere!

The best part? You're going to ask Claude to do it for you. That's what AI is for! 🤖✨

🤖 Step 1 — Ask Claude to build your page

Do this in Claude.ai

Go to claude.ai, start a new chat, and paste this prompt. Fill in YOUR details where it says [BRACKETS]:

Make me a personal webpage about me! My name is: [YOUR NAME] My favourite colour is: [A COLOUR] My favourite things are: [2 OR 3 THINGS YOU LOVE] My team is: [GIRLS or BOYS] Make it colourful, fun and full of emojis. Add a cool animation to the background. At the bottom write "Built with AI 🚀" When you're done, give me the complete code as a single HTML file.
💡 The more you describe, the cooler it gets! Tell Claude your favourite colour, show, food, sport — anything! The clearer your words, the better your page will look.

💾 Step 2 — Ask Claude to save it as a file

Still in Claude.ai

Once Claude has made your page, ask it to save the code as a file you can download. Copy and send this message in the same chat:

Can you give me that as a file I can download? Save it as mypage.html

Claude will give you a download button or a file to save. Click it and save the file somewhere easy to find — like your Desktop or Downloads folder.

👀 Look for a file called mypage.html in your downloads. That's your whole webpage in one tiny file!
Can't find a download button? Ask Claude: "Can you put the code in an artifact so I can copy it?" — then copy all the code and paste it into a new text file, saving it as mypage.html.

🚀 Step 3 — Drag it onto the internet!

✅ This is the magic bit

This is the coolest part. We use a free website called Netlify Drop. You just drag your file onto the page and it goes live on the internet instantly — no accounts, no passwords, nothing!

  • 1
    Open Netlify Drop Go to app.netlify.com/drop — you'll see a big page with a dotted box on it.
  • 2
    Find your mypage.html file Open your Downloads folder (or wherever you saved it) so you can see the file.
  • 3
    Drag the file into the dotted box Click and hold on your mypage.html file, then drag it across to the Netlify Drop page and drop it into the dotted box.
📄 ➡️ 🟦

Drag mypage.html into the dotted box on Netlify Drop

  • 4
    Wait about 10 seconds... Netlify will spin for a moment, then — boom! Your page is live on the internet! 🎉
  • 5
    Copy your link and share it! Netlify will show you a link that looks something like happy-rainbow-123.netlify.app. Copy it and send it to the family WhatsApp — anyone can click it and see your page!
🎉

You just built and launched a real website!

Most adults don't know how to do this. You just used AI to build a webpage and put it on the internet. That's actually incredible.

✨ Want to make it even better?

Easy! Go back to Claude in the same chat and ask it to make changes. Then download the new file and drag it onto Netlify Drop again — your link stays the same but the page updates!

Here are some things to ask Claude:

Add something new:

Add a section called "My Favourite Things" with a list of my top 5 favourites. Put a big emoji next to each one. Give me the full updated file.

Change the look:

Make the background a dark night sky with animated shooting stars. Keep everything else the same. Give me the full updated file.

Add a countdown to the hackathon:

Add a countdown timer to my page that counts down to April 4th 2026 at 6:30pm. Label it "Hackathon in..." and make it look exciting. Give me the full updated file.
🔑 The secret: Always say "give me the full updated file" at the end of your message to Claude, so it gives you the whole thing ready to drag onto Netlify again.
