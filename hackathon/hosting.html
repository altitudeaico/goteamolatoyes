Here's the idea

You already know how to ask Claude to build your webpage. Now you're going to ask Claude to put it online too!

To do this, Claude needs a special secret key (called a token) that lets it add your page to the family website. Uncle B will give you this key.

Once you have it — you just paste it into Claude and Claude handles everything. You don't need to understand how it works. That's the magic of AI! ✨

🗝️ Before you start — you need these two things from Uncle B:

  • Your secret token (a long code that starts with ghp_)
  • Confirmation that your account is set up ✅

Don't have these yet? Send Uncle B a message on WhatsApp and ask for your deploy token!

🤖 Step 1 — Build your page with Claude

Go to claude.ai, start a new chat, and paste this prompt. Fill in YOUR details:

Make me a personal webpage about me! My name is: [YOUR NAME] My favourite colour is: [A COLOUR] My favourite things are: [2 OR 3 THINGS YOU LOVE] My team is: [GIRLS or BOYS] Make it colourful, fun and full of emojis. Add a cool animation to the background. At the bottom write "Built with AI at Code Quest 🚀"
💡 The more detail the better! Tell Claude your favourite show, food, sport — anything. The clearer your words, the cooler your page.

🗝️ Step 2 — Give Claude the secret key

In the same Claude chat (don't start a new one!), send this message. You'll need to swap in your real token where it says [YOUR TOKEN]:

Now I want you to put this page online for me. Here are your instructions: [PASTE THE CONTENTS OF DEPLOY.md HERE] My name is: [YOUR FIRST NAME] My token is: [YOUR TOKEN — starts with ghp_] Please deploy my page now and give me the link when it's done.
⚠️ Keep your token secret! Don't share it with friends or post it anywhere. It's like a password — only use it in Claude.
🤔 What is DEPLOY.md? It's a set of instructions that tells Claude exactly how to put your page online. Uncle B will send it to you along with your token. Just copy and paste its contents into the message above.

⏳ Step 3 — Wait about 1 minute

Claude will do all the work — you'll see it thinking and then it will say something like:

✅ Your page is online! It will be live in about 1 minute at:
altitudeaico.github.io/goteamolatoyes/hackathon/yourname.html
Share that link with your family! 🎉

That's it — you're done! Copy your link and send it to the family WhatsApp group. 🎊

🎉

You just launched a real website!

You described what you wanted, Claude built it, and Claude put it online. That's real AI-powered development — and you just did it!

✨ Want to update your page?

Easy! In the same Claude chat, just ask for a change and then say "deploy it again please". Claude will update your page and the link stays exactly the same.

Try asking Claude things like:

Add a section called "My Top 5 Favourite Things" to my page with big emojis. Then deploy it again please.
Make the background a dark night sky with animated shooting stars. Then deploy it again please.
Add a countdown timer that counts down to 4th April 2026 at 6:30pm. Label it "Hackathon starts in..." and make it look exciting. Then deploy it again please.
🔑 The trick: Always end your message with "then deploy it again please" — that tells Claude to both make the change AND put the updated version online automatically.
